about me / contact
i'm mayra, a developer with a background in fine arts. i specialize in visual and interaction design, research and data analysis, and front and back-end development. i also have proficiency in JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, and JQuery. i like to resolve design problems, and enjoy finding new ways to tackle complex issues. i have a self-driven work-ethic that is detail-oriented, creative, and adaptable, which makes me a valuable asset to any team. i accept commissions, and when i'm not working on those, reading, or studying, i spend my free time having exestential crises' throughout my day. want to fix that? i am currently for-hire! explore my page to see my work, and feel free to reach out!
